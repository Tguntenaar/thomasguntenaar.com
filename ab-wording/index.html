/ 01 — The Thesis

TG / After Andreessen 2026 ⟶
— 01

The internet made information free. Every fact humanity knew collapsed into a search box. But access to facts was never the same as judgment — and judgment stayed scarce.

— 02

A book copies for nothing. A doctor, a lawyer, a master engineer takes decades to make and can't be cloned. Scarcity of expertise was the oldest bottleneck in the economy.

— 03

That bottleneck is now breaking. The cost of a fixed unit of machine intelligence is falling toward zero, on the same curve the smartphone rode — only faster.

— 04

The internet gave everyone access to knowledge. This gives everyone access to expertise — a categorically larger event.

— 05

The work doesn't disappear. It moves up the stack — from producing the analysis to deciding what it's for, which judgment to trust, and who owns the outcome.
